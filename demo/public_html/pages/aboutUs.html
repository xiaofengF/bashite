About Us

Founded in 1995, Baishite is a manufacturer specializing in the entertainment equipment and fitness products. Our factory covers an area of 10,000 square meters, equipped with advanced production machines. 8 technicians and over 200 workers ensure our high daily and annual output. New products can be promoted and developed into the market within every two months. ISO9001 and CE certificates were approved in 2003. Robots are installed to weld the machine perfectly. A wide range of fitness products are made by us such as stepper, AB fitness, dumbbell and some mini entertainment vehicles. They are popular in domestic market as well as foreign market due to quality and variability. We sincerely hope to create a wonderful future with our customers.
